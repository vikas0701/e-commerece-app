7️⃣ When to Use REST vs Kafka (Very Important)
REST (Synchronous)

Login

Product search

Get cart

Validate user

Kafka (Asynchronous Events)

Order placed

Payment completed

Inventory updated

Send notification

📌 Golden rule (interview-ready):

REST for queries, Kafka for business events.
